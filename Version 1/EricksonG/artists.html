SEMATARY

Sematary is a rap artist who makes music heavily inspired by black metal,
horror movies, and trap era artists like Chief Keef. He samples lots of black
metal songs from bands like Darkthrone, Woods Of Desolation, and Burzum.
He is heavily involved with Antifa, pro LGBTQ, and Anti-Christian themes.

If you like Black Metal, Rap, or Horror you should check out his music.

BLADEE

Bladee is a rap artist who makes music heavily inspired by 2000s era aesthetics,
but to say thats strictly what his style is would be an understatement. His style
is truly unique to himself, with a decade+ long career spanning multiple sounds
through evil and dark trap all the way to spiritual and bright synth based songs.
He has garnered a cult following along the way, and is a must to check out.

If you like Yung Lean, 2000s aesthetics or rap in general you should check out his music.

SKRILLEX

Skrillex is an electronic artist who makes music ranging from tons of genres,
starting off in an emo band and eventually making electronic music.
he has 9 grammys, and has collaborated and produced for artists like Kanye West,
Lady Gaga, Justin Bieber, Ed Sheeran, and many more.
He has done almost every electronic subgenre such as dubstep, house, ambient,
trap, riddim, and many non electronic genres as well.
His career has spanned 20+ years, and isnt slowing down any time soon.


If you like electronic/dance or Bladee (He has many collabs with him) then you
should check his music out.

DARKTHRONE

Darkthrone is a swedish black/death metal band, and is one of the biggest black metal
bands of all time. They have worked with huge figures in the BM scene, and have left
an impacting legacy on the world of metal music.

If you like metal, black metal, the bands Mayhem or Bathory, or heavy music in general, you should check out their music.
